FAQ's







1. Is it necessary to sign up/log in for ordering food ?
Yes, it is mandatory to create an account and have it logged in for ordering food from the website.

2. From how many restaurants can i order food using Yummy's website ?
Currently only 1 restaurant is available for ordering food using this website. But this can change in the future so make sure to verify your e-mail so that you can get notified when any changes occur in the website.

3. How do i add food items to my cart ?
Users can access the menu of the restaurant using the "Menu" button, from menu users can select/deselect the food items. Selected items will be added to the cart and quantity of the items can be changed in "Cart" page.

4. Can i pay the bill using my debit card ?
Yes, cards like debit cards, credit cards, ATM cards are all accepted as the mode of payment.

5. Can i cancel my order after making the payment ?
Sorry! But currently order cancellation is not available for online food orders.

6. Can i change my account details ?
Yes, changing account details like username and password can be changed.

7. How can i contact the restaurant ?
At the bottom part of the webpages, our e-mail and contact no. are mentioned, you can contact us using that.

8. Can i repeat my previous order ?
Yes, once you place an order, your cart will by-default contain the items from your previous order.
