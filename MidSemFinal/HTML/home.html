About Us

Launched in Bangalore, Yummy has grown from a home project to one of the largest food aggregators in the Karnataka. We are present in 50+ cities in Karnataka, enabling our vision of better food for more people by extending our reach. We not only connect people to food in every context but work closely with restaurants to enable a sustainable ecosystem.
